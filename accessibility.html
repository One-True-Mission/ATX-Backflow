Accessibility Statement

We want everyone to be able to use this website. Here is where we stand and how to reach us.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

ATX BackFlow is committed to making our website accessible to as many people as possible, including people who use assistive technology such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or screen magnification.

What We Do

We build this website with accessibility in mind, and we work toward conformance with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA where reasonably achievable. Steps we take include:

  • Using clear, readable text with strong color contrast
  • Providing descriptive alternative text on meaningful images
  • Building navigation that works with a keyboard, not just a mouse
  • Using semantic structure and labels so assistive technology can interpret the page
  • Designing layouts that adapt to phones, tablets, and desktops
  • Respecting reduced-motion settings for visitors who prefer less animation

Ongoing Effort

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time task. We review the website periodically and make improvements as we are able. Some content or features may not yet be fully accessible, and we appreciate your patience as we continue to improve.

Need Help, or Found a Problem?

If you have trouble using any part of this website, or you run into a barrier that keeps you from getting the information you need, please tell us. We will do our best to help you directly and to fix the issue.

You can also reach us the old-fashioned way: call us and we will gladly walk you through anything or take your request over the phone.
