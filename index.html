Who We Are

The Quiet Work That Keeps Drinking Water Clean.

ATX BackFlow is a small, certified team built around one job done right: making sure your backflow prevention assembly does what it was installed to do. We test it, we document it, we file the paperwork with your water authority, and when something fails, we fix it instead of selling you a replacement.

We've been on the ground in the greater Austin area for years. From standard half-inch domestic preventers on irrigation lines to twelve-inch reduced pressure assemblies on commercial fire systems, the range of what we work on is wide. The standard we hold ourselves to is not.

What We Do

Testing, Repair, and Rebuild.

Three services, one focus. Backflow prevention assemblies that pass the test and protect the public water supply. Anything bigger than that, we coordinate with trusted partners.

01

Annual Testing

Certified annual testing for residential and commercial backflow prevention assemblies. We complete the test, document the results, and file the report with your water authority on your behalf.

  • Irrigation and pool
  • Restaurants and hotels
  • Commercial buildings
  • Reports filed for you
02

Repair

When an assembly fails the test, replacement isn't always the answer. We repair what can be repaired and explain what can't, so you're paying for the right work and not a markup.

  • Failed test diagnosis
  • Component replacement
  • Re-test after repair
  • Honest assessments
03

Rebuild

Older assemblies often have years of life left in them with a proper rebuild. We strip them down, replace the worn internals, reseat everything, and certify the rebuild like a new install.

  • Full internal rebuild
  • OEM-spec parts
  • Up to 12-inch commercial
  • Certified after completion

Commercial Systems

Built for the Big Stuff.

Restaurants, hotels, medical buildings, fire suppression systems. Commercial backflow work means tighter compliance windows, larger assemblies, and zero tolerance for missed reports. We schedule testing on your operating hours, document everything, and keep your property in compliance with the City of Austin and surrounding water authorities.

For fire system testing, riser inspections, and pump flow tests, we coordinate with trusted partners so you can book the whole job through one number.

How It Works

Four Steps. No Surprises.

01

Schedule

Call, text, or fill out the form on our schedule page. We confirm a window that works for your property.

02

Test

A certified technician arrives on time, performs the test, and walks you through the result on the spot.

03

Repair if Needed

If the assembly fails, we explain why, quote the repair or rebuild, and get your approval before any work.

04

File the Report

We submit your test report to the appropriate water authority. You get a copy. Done until next year.
